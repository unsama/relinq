## Who We Are

Relinq is a 22-year IT solutions provider specializing in wholesale hardware distribution, corporate maintenance contracts, and R2-certified device refurbishing. Since 2004, we have served over 500 corporate clients across the region, delivering enterprise-grade technology without the enterprise-grade price tag.

## What Sets Us Apart

We are an R2-certified importer, meaning every refurbished device we deliver meets internationally recognized environmental and data security standards. Our partnerships with global brands — including Dell, HP, and Apple — allow us to source premium hardware at competitive prices and pass those savings directly to our clients.

## Our Commitment

We back every transaction with our **3-day no-hassle refund policy**, because we believe trust is built through accountability. From a single laptop to a fleet of 500 workstations, our team treats every order with the same attention and expertise that has kept our clients coming back for over two decades.
